About Us

Our Story

Bharat Bhajan Buffet has stood on this ground for over 50 years.
When Tum Hi Ho first came to the United States, it was a where homecooked indian
meals were brief respites from long days of hard work, and for most people,
they were rare luxuries. Tum Hi Ho and his wife, Samajavaragamana, decided to make
their living selling cheap homecooked meals from their very home, the foundations
of which our restaurant was built on. As they dedicated their lives to putting
flavor in the lives of others, we strive to do the same, preserving our Indian
culure through our exquisite homecooked meals.
